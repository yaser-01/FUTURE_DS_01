Key Business Insights
1. **Profitability by Region:** The **West Region** is our strongest performer with a 21.9% profit margin, while the **Central Region** is currently operating at a loss due to heavy discounting in the "Office Supplies" category.
2. **Category Performance:** **Technology** (specifically Copiers) drives the highest profit per unit, despite having lower sales volume than Furniture.
3. **Inventory Recommendations:** Focus on stocking high-margin items like **Phones and Accessories** in the East and West regions to maximize Q4 revenue.
